How the protocol works

01

Register your hive

Each beekeeper in the network registers their hive locations, honey varieties, and certifications. Identity is verified off-chain. Records are anchored on Polygon. A producer DID is issued — your permanent, portable identifier on the protocol.

02

Record each harvest

At extraction, producers log the batch: GPS coordinates, harvest date, weight, lab test results. Documents are stored on IPFS. A hash of the full record is written to the chain. The jar gets a QR code linked to the immutable record. Nothing can be changed retroactively.

03

Anyone can verify

Scan the QR. The record resolves instantly — beekeeper identity, harvest location, test results, chain confirmation. Retailers can integrate via API. Consumers can verify in two seconds on their phone. The proof is public. No login required.
