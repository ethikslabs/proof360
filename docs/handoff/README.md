## What Proof360 Is

Proof360 is the founder entry point. It cold-reads a company, turns observed trust gaps into an explainable briefing, and starts the commercial path toward vendor, investor, insurance, enterprise, and Ethiks360 relationship surfaces.

The current product direction is not "founder questionnaire" or "customer relationship management (CRM) system." It is a private founder memory kernel: memory is canonical, and the visible Company Profile is the first reconstructed projection.

## Product Boundary

- Proof360 is the entry and memory surface.
- CORPUS is the shared evidence layer.
- VERITAS proves claims where third-party verification is needed.
- FORUM surfaces commercial routes.
- Ethiks360 is the later external relationship destination.

## Do Not Change Casually

- Do not make unknowns look known.
- Do not turn projections into facts.
- Do not bypass the founder-memory promotion path.
- Do not expose Ethiks360 sharing in Proof360 V1.
- Do not create a founder-memory Postgres migration for V1; the active plan is JSON-first.
